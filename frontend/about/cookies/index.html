How We Use Cookies on Our Website

Our website employs essential cookies to ensure its proper functioning and to enhance your user experience. These cookies are necessary for basic functionalities such as tracking user interactions, managing your selected theme, and remembering your consent to our cookie policy. Here's a detailed explanation of the types of cookies we use and their purposes:


UUID Cookie

We use a UUID (Universally Unique Identifier) cookie to differentiate between users and track their interactions with our website. This cookie helps us remember whether you have upvoted, downvoted, liked, or disliked content on our site. By using a unique identifier, we can provide a personalized experience without collecting any personal information such as your name, email, or date of birth.


Theme Cookie

Our website allows you to choose your preferred theme for a customized viewing experience. We use a theme cookie to store your theme preference so that your choice is remembered every time you visit our site. This ensures a consistent and enjoyable experience tailored to your visual preferences.


Consent Cookie

To comply with privacy regulations and to respect your choices, we use a consent cookie to record that you have acknowledged our cookie banner. When you click "OK" on the cookie banner, this cookie is set to remember your consent, preventing the banner from appearing every time you visit our site. This cookie ensures a smoother and less intrusive browsing experience.


Transparency and Control


We are committed to transparency regarding the use of cookies on our website. While these essential cookies are crucial for the website's functionality and user experience, we want to ensure you are fully informed about their use. Essential cookies do not require explicit consent under privacy regulations; however, we provide clear information on their purpose and functionality.
